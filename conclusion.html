Conclusion

In conclusion, the data shows a significant correlation between the amount of time teenagers spend on the internet and their academic performance, with variations between genders. Girls who dedicate more time to the internet tend to maintain good grades, while boys who spend more time online often struggle academically due to activities like online gaming. This is strengthened by the different graphs from the male and female groups, where, you can see t he differences and the similarities.
